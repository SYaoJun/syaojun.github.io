I finished my first half marathon last weekend.
I’m a little proud. 
When I told my badminton buddies that I kept a pace of about six minutes per kilometer, they were pretty impressed.

My first 10km run was when I was 25. 
My second 10km run didn’t happen until I turned 30. 
For almost five years, I didn’t attempt any long-distance running, let alone think about taking part in a half marathon.
